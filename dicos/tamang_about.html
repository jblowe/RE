About

This is an early prototype of an online dictionary for the Tamang language of Nepal as spoken in the village of Risiangku (Lisankhu) in Sindhupalchok district, Bagmati province. It works on both mobile devices and desktop computers. It is built as a single standalone HTML page: it requires a web browser but does not require a connection to the internet.

Please send comments, corrections, indeed any feedback, to the creators. We would love to hear what you think.

Two facilities for searching the dictionary are provided:

  • Point-and-click searching using the ordered initial "letters" shown in the navigation bar.
  • A "search box" that performs an instantaneous free text search of the entire dictionary.

The dictionary contains {entry_count} entries, and most have definitions in three languages: Nepali (in Devanagari with transliteration), French, and English.
